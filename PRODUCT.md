# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Demand (Customers):** Home owners, renters, and office/condo managers in Nong Khai looking for quick, reliable local maintenance services (technicians, maids, AC repair, etc.).
- **Supply (Service Providers):** Local technicians (plumbers, electricians, bricklayers, AC technicians) and maids in Nong Khai seeking to find local clients and grow their business.

## Product Purpose
A local service-matching platform that connects Nong Khai residents directly with verified local service providers. It is free for users, takes 0% commission, and generates revenue through featured ad placements for service providers.

## Brand Personality
- **Local & Friendly:** Speaks the local language, accessible, and community-focused.
- **Trustworthy & Clean:** High emphasis on verification (ID check, work photos) and authentic reviews.
- **Straightforward (Direct):** Direct line of contact (LINE OA, in-app chat, direct phone), no middleman friction.

## Anti-references
- Saturated AI-style SaaS landing pages with cream/biscuit backgrounds and generic illustrations.
- Overly complex dashboard designs that hide technician contact info behind paywalls.
- High-commission platforms that inflate service prices.

## Design Principles
1. **Trust-First Verification:** Make the "Verified Badge" and technician's past work photos prominent to build trust in a local community.
2. **Direct Contact Clarity:** Ensure contact buttons (LINE OA, Chat, Call) are bold, accessible, and require zero training to use.
3. **No-Nonsense Local Search:** The homepage search should be fast and simple, focusing on category and distance (via PostGIS).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Large, high-contrast typography readable by older homeowners and field technicians.
- WCAG AA contrast compliance (especially on buttons and statuses).
- Responsive mobile-first design, optimized for low-bandwidth mobile connections.
